Single Origin Selection

Choose from our rotating selection of single-origin beans for your manual brew:

  • Sumatra Mandheling - Earthy, herbal, low acidity with chocolate notes
  • Bali Kintamani - Bright citrus acidity with tropical fruit notes
  • Java Preanger - Balanced with floral aroma and caramel sweetness
  • Toraja Sapan - Complex with wine-like acidity and berry notes
  • Aceh Gayo - Rich with brown sugar sweetness and spice notes
