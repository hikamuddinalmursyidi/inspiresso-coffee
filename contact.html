Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you offer tours of your roastery?

Yes, we offer guided tours of our roastery every Saturday at 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM. Tours include a coffee tasting and a behind-the-scenes look at our roasting process. Reservations are recommended and can be made through our WhatsApp or by email.

Can I host a private event at your café?

Absolutely! Our space is available for private events, workshops, and meetings. We can accommodate groups of up to 30 people and offer customized coffee experiences for your guests. Contact us for availability and pricing.

Do you ship your coffee beans internationally?

Yes, we ship our coffee beans to select countries internationally. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Contact us directly for international shipping inquiries and we'll provide you with the necessary information.

How can I become a wholesale partner?

We offer wholesale partnerships for cafés, restaurants, and businesses interested in serving or selling our coffee. Please email wholesale@inspiressocoffee.com with details about your business, and our wholesale team will contact you with more information.
