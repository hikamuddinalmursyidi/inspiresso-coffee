Our Story

Discover the journey behind Inspiresso Coffee and Roastery

From Bean to Cup

Founded in 2015 by coffee enthusiasts Andi Wijaya and Sari Putri, Inspiresso Coffee and Roastery was born from a passion for exceptional Indonesian coffee and a desire to share it with the world.

Our journey began with travels across the Indonesian archipelago – from the highlands of Aceh in Sumatra to the volcanic soils of Bali and the remote mountains of Papua. We connected with local farmers, learned about traditional cultivation methods, and discovered the incredible diversity of flavors that Indonesian coffee offers.

What started as a small roastery with a tiny café has grown into a beloved destination for coffee lovers seeking authentic Indonesian coffee experiences. Today, we continue to work directly with farmers across Indonesia, ensuring ethical sourcing practices while delivering outstanding coffee to our customers.

Our Coffee Process

How we transform coffee cherries into exceptional cups

1

Direct Sourcing

We travel to coffee-growing regions across Indonesia, building relationships with small-scale farmers and cooperatives. By sourcing directly, we ensure fair prices for farmers and access to the finest beans.

2

Careful Selection

Our green beans undergo rigorous quality control before entering our roastery. We select only the best beans, with specific flavor characteristics that represent their region of origin.

3

Artisanal Roasting

Each batch is roasted to perfection in our carefully controlled small-batch roaster. Our master roaster develops unique profiles for each bean variety to highlight its individual characteristics.

4

Precision Brewing

Whether in our café or through our brewing guides for home use, we ensure precise brewing methods that bring out the best flavor in each cup of coffee we serve.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Inspiresso, sustainability isn't just a buzzword—it's a core part of our business model. We believe that great coffee should benefit everyone involved in its journey, from farm to cup.

Fair Partnerships

We pay premium prices to our coffee farmers, well above market rates, to ensure they can invest in their farms and communities.

Environmental Practices

We support shade-grown coffee cultivation that preserves forest canopies and promotes biodiversity in coffee-growing regions.

Waste Reduction

Our café implements comprehensive recycling and composting programs, and we offer discounts for customers who bring reusable cups.
